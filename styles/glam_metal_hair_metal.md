# Glam Metal / Hair Metal

**Style ID:** `glam_metal_hair_metal`  
**Active Window:** 1983–1989  
**Origin:** Los Angeles, USA  
**Decade(s):** 1980s

## Overview

Teased hair, tight denim, spandex, eyeliner, and stage-ready neon theatrics built for MTV spectacle and arena rock visibility.

**Cultural context:** Music video era; commercialized rebellion; Sunset Strip nightlife economy.

**Aesthetic opposites / contrasts:** Grunge; minimalism (90s).
