# Club Kids / Camp Clubwear

**Style ID:** `club_kids_camp_clubwear`  
**Active Window:** 1985–1996  
**Origin:** New York City, USA  
**Decade(s):** 1980s–90s

## Overview

New York nightlife as competitive costume sport. Club Kids (the actual named group around Michael Alig, Amanda Lepore, RuPaul, and others) used extreme costuming — foam creatures, latex, full-body paint, drag conventions pushed to conceptual extremes — as the cover charge for entry into Manhattan's nightlife circuit. The look is the opposite of minimalism.

**Cultural context:** Post-AIDS NYC nightlife; queer/drag tradition; celebrity-of-self culture predating social media.

**Aesthetic opposites / contrasts:** Minimalism 90s; preppy; grunge
