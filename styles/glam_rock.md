# Glam Rock

**Style ID:** `glam_rock`  
**Active Window:** 1971–1975  
**Origin:** London, UK  
**Decade(s):** 1970s

## Overview

Rock-star theatricality taken to its most elaborate expression. Platform boots, glitter makeup, jumpsuits, capes, skin-tight satin trousers. Glam rock explicitly blurs gender presentation — David Bowie, Marc Bolan, and Gary Glitter all make masculinity strange. The look is about performance persona first, street style second.

**Cultural context:** Post-hippie disillusionment; rock stardom as visual performance; early gender non-conformism in mass media.

**Aesthetic opposites / contrasts:** Punk's anti-glamour; hippie naturalness
