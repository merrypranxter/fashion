# Anarcho / Celtic Punk

**Style ID:** `anarcho_celtic_punk`  
**Active Window:** 1977–1982  
**Origin:** UK / Ireland diaspora circuits  
**Decade(s):** 1970s–1980s

## Overview

Punk splinters combining stenciled politics, DIY patches, and either vegan anti-leather choices (anarcho) or Celtic heritage pieces like kilts and grandfather shirts.

**Cultural context:** Ideological fragmentation of punk; local identity signaling.

**Aesthetic opposites / contrasts:** Preppy / Ivy; Power Dressing.
