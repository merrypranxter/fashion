# Skate Street Style

**Style ID:** `skate_street`  
**Active Window:** 1989–1999  
**Origin:** Los Angeles / New York City, USA  
**Decade(s):** 1990s

## Overview

Skateboarding's visual culture as fashion system. Baggy cargo shorts or jeans, oversized tees, hooded sweatshirts, flannel shirts — durable clothing for street activity. Brands like Vans, DC, and Stüssy were native to the scene; the look cross-pollinated with hip-hop in the late 80s/early 90s.

**Cultural context:** Suburban/urban youth culture; DIY skate zines; West Coast alternative to East Coast hip-hop aesthetic.

**Aesthetic opposites / contrasts:** Power dressing; preppy
