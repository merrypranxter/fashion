# Wrap Dress Revolution

**Style ID:** `wrap_dress_revolution`  
**Active Window:** 1974–1979  
**Origin:** New York City, USA  
**Decade(s):** 1970s

## Overview

Jersey dresses that wrap and tie at the waist, balancing polish, comfort, and movement. The piece became a day-to-night uniform for modern professional life.

**Cultural context:** Women entering white-collar spaces; demand for adaptable practical elegance.

**Aesthetic opposites / contrasts:** Power dressing; minimalist (90s).
