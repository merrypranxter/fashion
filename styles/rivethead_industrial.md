# Rivethead / Industrial Fashion

**Style ID:** `rivethead_industrial`  
**Active Window:** 1985–1999  
**Origin:** US/Europe industrial club scenes  
**Decade(s):** 1980s–1990s

## Overview

Military and workwear-derived silhouettes with combat boots, hardware, flight jackets, and utilitarian goggles in muted palettes.

**Cultural context:** Industrial/EBM scene growth; mechanization anxiety; post-nuclear visual language.

**Aesthetic opposites / contrasts:** Bohemian revival 90s; preppy / ivy.
