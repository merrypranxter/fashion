# Bodycon

**Style ID:** `bodycon`  
**Active Window:** 1980–1989  
**Origin:** Paris / global nightlife  
**Decade(s):** 1980s

## Overview

Body-conscious stretch garments in Lycra, spandex, and bandage constructions that closely traced the torso and hips.

**Cultural context:** Fitness culture; new stretch textiles; club and media visibility.

**Aesthetic opposites / contrasts:** Japanese avant-garde / deconstruction; grunge.
