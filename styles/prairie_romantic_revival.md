# Prairie / Romantic Revival

**Style ID:** `prairie_romantic_revival`  
**Active Window:** 1970–1975  
**Origin:** United Kingdom / United States  
**Decade(s):** 1970s

## Overview

Victorian- and Edwardian-inflected midi/maxi dresses with lace, smocking, floral prints, and ruffles. A soft, nostalgic silhouette countered urban and economic anxiety.

**Cultural context:** Economic uncertainty; nostalgia markets; heritage retail expansion.

**Aesthetic opposites / contrasts:** Disco / nightlife glamour; bodycon.
