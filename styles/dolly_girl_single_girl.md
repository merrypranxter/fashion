# Dolly Girl / Single Girl

**Style ID:** `dolly_girl_single_girl`  
**Active Window:** 1963–1966  
**Origin:** London, UK  
**Decade(s):** 1960s

## Overview

Youth boutique fashion centered on miniskirts, shift dresses, saturated tights, flat shoes, and geometric cuts. It translated Mod precision into a distinctly young womenswear language tied to mobility and independence.

**Cultural context:** Youthquake retail; women entering urban youth culture as direct consumers.

**Aesthetic opposites / contrasts:** Ladylike elegance; preppy / ivy.
