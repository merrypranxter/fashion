# Power Dressing

**Style ID:** `power_dressing`  
**Active Window:** 1983–1989  
**Origin:** New York City, USA / London, UK  
**Decade(s):** 1980s

## Overview

Fashion as social technology for women entering corporate environments. The padded shoulder is the key signature element — a visual expansion of physical authority. The look draws on men's suiting grammar (double-breasted, structured tailoring) but amplifies it: bolder accessories, more dramatic color. Armani, Donna Karan, and Thierry Mugler are the defining designers.

**Cultural context:** Women entering high-powered workplaces; Reagan-era corporate culture; Dynasty and Dallas TV aesthetics.

**Aesthetic opposites / contrasts:** Club/anti-fashion; hippie looseness; minimalism (as reaction)
