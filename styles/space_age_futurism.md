# Space Age Futurism

**Style ID:** `space_age_futurism`  
**Active Window:** 1964–1968  
**Origin:** Paris, France / London, UK  
**Decade(s):** 1960s

## Overview

Runway-level response to the space race. André Courrèges and Pierre Cardin produced structured geometric clothing in white, silver, and primary colors. White go-go boots, architectural coatdresses, minimal hardware. The look is optimistic about synthetic modernity.

**Cultural context:** Space race; synthetic materials boom; modernist architecture as aesthetic parallel.

**Aesthetic opposites / contrasts:** Hippie handmade eclecticism
