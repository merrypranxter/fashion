# Preppy / Ivy

**Style ID:** `preppy_ivy`  
**Active Window:** 1980–1999  
**Origin:** Northeast United States  
**Decade(s):** 1980s–90s

## Overview

The long-running aspiration aesthetic of American collegiate and elite culture, codified in the 1980 publication of The Official Preppy Handbook and Ralph Lauren's mass-market translation. Oxford button-downs, navy blazers, khakis, loafers, sweaters-over-shoulders — the grammar of old-money American style.

**Cultural context:** WASP elite culture; yuppie aspiration in the 1980s; campus as style context.

**Aesthetic opposites / contrasts:** Punk; grunge; hip-hop streetwear
