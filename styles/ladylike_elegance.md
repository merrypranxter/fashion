# Ladylike Elegance

**Style ID:** `ladylike_elegance`  
**Active Window:** 1960–1964  
**Origin:** Paris / Washington, D.C. media echo  
**Decade(s):** 1960s

## Overview

A continuation of late-1950s formal daywear: boxy skirt suits, sheath dresses, pillbox hats, gloves, and pearls. The look signaled composure, social polish, and elite etiquette before youth-driven disruption accelerated mid-decade.

**Cultural context:** Postwar conformity; couture authority; diplomatic/media image-making.

**Aesthetic opposites / contrasts:** Mod; hippie / psychedelic bohemia.
