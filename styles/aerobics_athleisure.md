# Aerobics / Athleisure (80s)

**Style ID:** `aerobics_athleisure`  
**Active Window:** 1981–1985  
**Origin:** Los Angeles / New York City, USA  
**Decade(s):** 1980s

## Overview

The fitness boom translated into street style. Lycra and spandex leotards, high-cut tights, leg warmers, oversized sweatshirts — worn to aerobics class and then to the supermarket. Jane Fonda's workout videos are the canonical media reference. The look is body-celebratory and bright.

**Cultural context:** 1980s fitness culture; body-image media; the leisure-wear mainstreaming.

**Aesthetic opposites / contrasts:** Goth; power dressing; Japanese avant-garde
