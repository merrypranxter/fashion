# Deathrock

**Style ID:** `deathrock`  
**Active Window:** 1980–1989  
**Origin:** Los Angeles, USA  
**Decade(s):** 1980s

## Overview

US gothic-punk variant blending black punk staples with camp horror cues, skeletal makeup, and theatrical macabre styling.

**Cultural context:** Post-punk mutation; horror cinema aesthetics; club-night performance identity.

**Aesthetic opposites / contrasts:** Preppy / ivy; minimalism (90s).
