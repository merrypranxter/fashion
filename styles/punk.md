# Punk

**Style ID:** `punk`  
**Active Window:** 1976–1978  
**Origin:** London, UK / New York City, USA  
**Decade(s):** 1970s

## Overview

The deliberate construction of offense as aesthetic. Vivienne Westwood and Malcolm McLaren's SEX/Seditionaries shop provided the canonical vocabulary: bondage trousers, safety pins, ripped tees, tartan, leather, metal hardware. The Met frames punk as an early manifestation of deconstructionist fashion and traces how couture repeatedly borrows punk's visual symbols.

**Cultural context:** Economic crisis in 1970s UK; anti-establishment politics; DIY culture; early post-modern semiotics.

**Aesthetic opposites / contrasts:** Disco glamour; preppy collegiate
