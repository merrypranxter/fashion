# 1940s Revival / Retro Nostalgia

**Style ID:** `forties_revival_retro_nostalgia`  
**Active Window:** 1971–1975  
**Origin:** Paris, France  
**Decade(s):** 1970s

## Overview

Broad shoulders, turban references, wedge footwear, and wartime-tailored echoes re-entered high fashion. The look mined interwar/WWII silhouettes for dramatic contrast with 60s youth minimalism.

**Cultural context:** Designer-led historical quotation; cyclical nostalgia during instability.

**Aesthetic opposites / contrasts:** Punk; grunge.
