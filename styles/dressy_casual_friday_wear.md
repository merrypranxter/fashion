# Dressy Casual / Friday Wear

**Style ID:** `dressy_casual_friday_wear`  
**Active Window:** 1995–1999  
**Origin:** US corporate offices  
**Decade(s):** 1990s

## Overview

Khakis, polos, knitwear, and unstructured blazers replacing strict weekday suiting. The look codified office informality without full streetwear adoption.

**Cultural context:** Dot-com workplace norms; retail campaigns around business-casual codes.

**Aesthetic opposites / contrasts:** Power dressing; punk.
