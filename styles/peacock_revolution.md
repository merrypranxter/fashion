# Peacock Revolution

**Style ID:** `peacock_revolution`  
**Active Window:** 1965–1975  
**Origin:** London, UK  
**Decade(s):** 1960s–1970s

## Overview

A flamboyant menswear break from sober tailoring: velvet, frills, paisley, military braiding, and saturated color. Masculinity became performative and decorative again.

**Cultural context:** Rock-star influence; anti-authoritarian youth culture; collapse of conservative menswear codes.

**Aesthetic opposites / contrasts:** Power dressing; minimalism (90s).
