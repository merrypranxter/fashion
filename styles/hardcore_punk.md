# Hardcore Punk

**Style ID:** `hardcore_punk`  
**Active Window:** 1980–1989  
**Origin:** US regional scenes (LA/DC/NY)  
**Decade(s):** 1980s

## Overview

Stripped-down anti-fashion uniform built for movement: cut-offs, plain tees, sneakers, shaved heads, and durable layers.

**Cultural context:** DIY ethics; anti-commercial stance; mosh-pit functionality.

**Aesthetic opposites / contrasts:** Power Dressing; Millennium Futurism (Y2K).
