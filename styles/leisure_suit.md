# Leisure Suit

**Style ID:** `leisure_suit`  
**Active Window:** 1972–1978  
**Origin:** United States  
**Decade(s):** 1970s

## Overview

Synthetic pastel two-piece suits with wide lapels worn casually with open-collar patterned shirts. It bridged formal suiting codes and relaxed nightlife color.

**Cultural context:** Polyester mass manufacturing; suburban casualization; disco spillover.

**Aesthetic opposites / contrasts:** Grunge; minimalism (90s).
