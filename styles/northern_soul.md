# Northern Soul

**Style ID:** `northern_soul`  
**Active Window:** 1970–1979  
**Origin:** Northern England, UK  
**Decade(s):** 1970s

## Overview

Function-first dancewear: high-waisted wide trousers, knitted tops, bowling shirts, and smooth-soled shoes for spins and glides. Club badges and patches signaled scene status.

**Cultural context:** All-night dance circuits; crate-digging music culture; working-class club identity.

**Aesthetic opposites / contrasts:** Punk; preppy / ivy.
