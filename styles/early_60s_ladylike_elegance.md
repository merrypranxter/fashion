# Early-60s Ladylike Elegance

**Style ID:** `early_60s_ladylike_elegance`  
**Active Window:** 1960–1964  
**Origin:** United States / Western Europe  
**Decade(s):** 1960s

## Overview

The polished continuation of late-1950s femininity before the youthquake fully took over: boxy suits, bracelet sleeves, sheath dresses, matching hats and gloves, tidy handbags, and controlled posture. This is the side of the early 1960s that gets erased when people flatten the whole decade into miniskirts.

**Cultural context:** postwar prosperity; society dressing; First Lady influence; department-store polish.

**Aesthetic opposites / contrasts:** Mod; hippie; punk.
