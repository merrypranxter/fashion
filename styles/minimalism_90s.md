# Minimalism (90s)

**Style ID:** `minimalism_90s`  
**Active Window:** 1990–1999  
**Origin:** New York City, USA / Paris, France  
**Decade(s):** 1990s

## Overview

The dominant luxury aesthetic of the 1990s, explicitly framed by MFIT as a reaction to 1980s excess, intensified by economic recession. Calvin Klein, Jil Sander, Helmut Lang, and early Prada produced clean column silhouettes, high-quality basics, quiet accessories. The aesthetic is about restraint as sophistication.

**Cultural context:** Recession economy making wealth display feel tactless; reaction to 1980s maximalism; emerging 'quiet luxury' discourse.

**Aesthetic opposites / contrasts:** Grunge; club kids; power dressing
