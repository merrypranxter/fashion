# Kinderwhore

**Style ID:** `kinderwhore`  
**Active Window:** 1991–1996  
**Origin:** Pacific Northwest / alt scenes  
**Decade(s):** 1990s

## Overview

Babydoll dresses, Mary Janes, barrettes, and smeared makeup combined innocence cues with abrasive punk distortion.

**Cultural context:** Feminist critique of the male gaze; alternative music scenes.

**Aesthetic opposites / contrasts:** Preppy / ivy; ladylike elegance.
