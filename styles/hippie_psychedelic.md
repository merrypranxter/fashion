# Hippie / Psychedelic Bohemia

**Style ID:** `hippie_psychedelic`  
**Active Window:** 1967–1973  
**Origin:** San Francisco, USA / London, UK (diffusion)  
**Decade(s):** 1960s–70s

## Overview

The dominant counterculture aesthetic: loose, layered, handmade, secondhand. The visual language draws from Eastern textiles (Indian prints, Afghan coats, kaftans), American folk craft (patchwork, crochet, fringe), and psychedelic color. The look deliberately signals rejection of consumer fashion systems.

**Cultural context:** Anti-Vietnam War movement; commune/festival culture; Woodstock as visual shorthand.

**Aesthetic opposites / contrasts:** Mod precision; power dressing
