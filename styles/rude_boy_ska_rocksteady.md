# Rude Boy (Ska / Rocksteady Era)

**Style ID:** `rude_boy_ska_rocksteady`  
**Active Window:** 1962–1968  
**Origin:** Kingston, Jamaica  
**Decade(s):** 1960s

## Overview

Jamaican street style centered on dancehall scene and tailored suiting as status performance. Sharp two-piece suits, pork pie hats, slim ties, loafers. The rude boy look is about precision and aspirational elegance — working-class youth claiming the visual grammar of professional authority.

**Cultural context:** Post-colonial Jamaica; dancehall scene; ska and rocksteady music culture.

**Aesthetic opposites / contrasts:** Hippie looseness
