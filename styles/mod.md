# Mod

**Style ID:** `mod`  
**Active Window:** 1958–1966  
**Origin:** London, UK  
**Decade(s):** 1960s

## Overview

Youth-driven modernism centered on London's Carnaby Street and King's Road boutiques. Clean geometric silhouettes, bold color blocking, above-knee hemlines. The style that made Mary Quant and John Stephen household names. Mod separates youth identity from adult fashion conventions through precision and graphic clarity rather than rebellion.

**Cultural context:** Post-war youth consumerism; boutique retail explosion; R&B and beat music as style drivers.

**Aesthetic opposites / contrasts:** Hippie eclecticism (late 1960s)
