# Beatnik-Influenced Couture

**Style ID:** `beatnik_couture`  
**Active Window:** 1960–1962  
**Origin:** Paris, France  
**Decade(s):** 1960s

## Overview

The earliest movement in the database — the moment when Left Bank bohemia and beat nonconformism started influencing Paris couture. Yves Saint Laurent's early Dior years are the canonical reference. The look is slim, dark, intellectual: black leather, lean tailoring, minimal jewelry.

**Cultural context:** Cold War intellectual culture; jazz clubs as social scene; countercultural attitudes filtered through high fashion's lens.

**Aesthetic opposites / contrasts:** Space Age futurism (1964), preppy/ivy (1980s)
