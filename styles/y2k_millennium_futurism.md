# Millennium Futurism (Y2K)

**Style ID:** `y2k_millennium_futurism`  
**Active Window:** 1998–2000  
**Origin:** Global (media-driven)  
**Decade(s):** 1990s–2000s

## Overview

The aesthetic anxiety and excitement of the millennium convergence. Runway shows pushed metallics, strapping, low-rise silhouettes, and black synthetics. The Matrix (1999) and runway spectacle converged on a shared visual: sleek, strapped, chrome, technical. The look is optimistic-anxious about technology.

**Cultural context:** Y2K panic; tech boom; global media saturation; turn-of-millennium runway spectacle.

**Aesthetic opposites / contrasts:** Britpop/Madchester retro casual; grunge
