# Goth

**Style ID:** `goth`  
**Active Window:** 1981–1995  
**Origin:** London, UK  
**Decade(s):** 1980s–90s

## Overview

Grew directly from post-punk and the same London club scenes that produced New Romantic. The goth aesthetic is darker and more consistent: black lace, velvet, leather, silver jewelry with romantic/macabre motifs (ankh, cross), pale makeup, dark lipstick. The look is about romantic darkness as world-view, not just fashion.

**Cultural context:** Post-punk subcultural depth; romantic literary tradition; Batcave club as scene hub.

**Aesthetic opposites / contrasts:** Disco glamour; preppy collegiate; aerobics bright athleisure
