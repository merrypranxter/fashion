# Gyaru

**Style ID:** `gyaru`  
**Active Window:** 1997–2000  
**Origin:** Tokyo, Japan  
**Decade(s):** 1990s

## Overview

Deliberately nonconforming youth style with bleached hair, deep tanning, dramatic makeup, and heavily customized school-inspired outfits.

**Cultural context:** Youth rebellion against conservative beauty norms and social expectations.

**Aesthetic opposites / contrasts:** Ladylike elegance; minimalist (90s).
