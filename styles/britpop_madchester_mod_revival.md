# Britpop / Madchester (Mod-Revival Casual)

**Style ID:** `britpop_madchester_mod_revival`  
**Active Window:** 1990–1997  
**Origin:** Manchester / London, UK  
**Decade(s):** 1990s

## Overview

The UK music scenes of Madchester (Stone Roses, Happy Mondays) and Britpop (Oasis, Blur, Pulp) produced a casual working-class swagger look: parka, polo shirt, straight jeans, desert boots. Oasis and their audience revived mod aesthetic codes — the parka as essential outerwear — through retro-sportswear mixing.

**Cultural context:** Working-class British cultural resurgence; Cool Britannia discourse; terrace culture and indie music crossover.

**Aesthetic opposites / contrasts:** Y2K futurism; American grunge
