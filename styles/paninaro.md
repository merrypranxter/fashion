# Paninaro

**Style ID:** `paninaro`  
**Active Window:** 1980–1989  
**Origin:** Milan, Italy  
**Decade(s):** 1980s

## Overview

Logo-forward affluent youth uniform centered on premium casual brands, puffer jackets, cuffed denim, and visible status accessories.

**Cultural context:** Post-ideological consumerism; brand worship; fast-food social hubs.

**Aesthetic opposites / contrasts:** Grunge; normcore minimal casual.
