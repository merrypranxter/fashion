# The Lean Silhouette

**Style ID:** `lean_silhouette`  
**Active Window:** 1970–1976  
**Origin:** UK/US pop culture  
**Decade(s):** 1970s

## Overview

Narrow, elongated lines in close-cut shirts, flared trousers, heeled boots, and waist-defining belts. The body line was slim, sensual, and often androgynous.

**Cultural context:** Post-peacock and glam influence; nightlife and celebrity image culture.

**Aesthetic opposites / contrasts:** Power dressing; hip-hop streetwear.
