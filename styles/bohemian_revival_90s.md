# Bohemian Revival (90s)

**Style ID:** `bohemian_revival_90s`  
**Active Window:** 1996–2000  
**Origin:** Global runway + high street  
**Decade(s):** 1990s

## Overview

A late-90s return of 70s-inspired layers, embroidery, relaxed skirts, and mixed artisanal textures as a softer alternative to strict minimalism.

**Cultural context:** Minimalism fatigue; heritage craft nostalgia; eclectic styling.

**Aesthetic opposites / contrasts:** Minimalism (90s); power dressing.
