# Op Art Fashion

**Style ID:** `op_art_fashion`  
**Active Window:** 1964–1968  
**Origin:** London / Paris  
**Decade(s):** 1960s

## Overview

High-contrast geometric prints and color blocking designed to create optical vibration and motion. Garments acted like moving graphic art, often in black-and-white or hard primary contrasts.

**Cultural context:** Mass-media appetite for bold visuals; crossover of fine art into apparel.

**Aesthetic opposites / contrasts:** Minimalism (90s); bohemian revival 90s.
