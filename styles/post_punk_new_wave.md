# Post-Punk / New Wave

**Style ID:** `post_punk_new_wave`  
**Active Window:** 1978–1983  
**Origin:** London, UK / New York City, USA  
**Decade(s):** 1970s–80s

## Overview

The more accessible but still art-inflected aftermath of punk. Personal styling becomes an act of self-curation. i-D magazine's 'Straight Up' documentary street portraits capture the look: angular shapes, thrift-store layering, DIY badges, graphic eyeliner, experimental hair. The style is not cohesive — it's about individual variation within an anti-mainstream stance.

**Cultural context:** Art-school culture; DIY music industry; fanzine culture; early music video era.

**Aesthetic opposites / contrasts:** Power dressing; preppy
