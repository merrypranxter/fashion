# Heroin Chic

**Style ID:** `heroin_chic`  
**Active Window:** 1993–1998  
**Origin:** Global editorial fashion  
**Decade(s):** 1990s

## Overview

A deliberately gaunt, pale, low-energy look with minimal styling and anti-glamour photography. It inverted 80s athletic supermodel ideals.

**Cultural context:** Recession-era anti-excess; editorial provocation; indie realism.

**Aesthetic opposites / contrasts:** Power dressing; glam metal / hair metal.
