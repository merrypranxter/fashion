# Cybergoth

**Style ID:** `cybergoth`  
**Active Window:** 1997–2000  
**Origin:** European club / rave circuits  
**Decade(s):** 1990s

## Overview

Industrial-rave fusion with UV neon, PVC, oversized platforms, synthetic hair extensions, and respirator/gas-mask motifs.

**Cultural context:** Rave culture plus dystopian performance styling; late-millennium club theatrics.

**Aesthetic opposites / contrasts:** Minimalism (90s); dressy casual / friday wear.
