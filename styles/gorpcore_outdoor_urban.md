# Gorpcore / Outdoor Urban

**Style ID:** `gorpcore_outdoor_urban`  
**Active Window:** 1990–2000  
**Origin:** US/Europe urban centers  
**Decade(s):** 1990s

## Overview

Technical outerwear—fleece, puffers, shell jackets, hiking footwear—recontextualized as everyday city style.

**Cultural context:** Utility-first dressing; climate pragmatism; street adoption of outdoor brands.

**Aesthetic opposites / contrasts:** Power dressing; sexy schoolgirl.
