# Two-Tone Ska Revival

**Style ID:** `two_tone_ska_revival`  
**Active Window:** 1979–1982  
**Origin:** Coventry, UK  
**Decade(s):** 1970s–80s

## Overview

A racially mixed British music scene that revived rude boy aesthetics with a monochrome graphic identity. The 2 Tone record label (The Specials, Madness, The Selecter) made the checkerboard pattern a political emblem of mixed-race unity. Sharp suits, pork pie hats, checked scarves.

**Cultural context:** Race relations in 1970s/80s UK; anti-racism politics; ska revival as cultural statement.

**Aesthetic opposites / contrasts:** Hippie eclecticism; power dressing
