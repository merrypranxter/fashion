# Disco / Nightlife Glamour

**Style ID:** `disco_glam`  
**Active Window:** 1974–1979  
**Origin:** New York City, USA  
**Decade(s):** 1970s

## Overview

The nightclub as fashion context: clothing designed for visibility on the dance floor under disco lighting. Halston's jersey and sequins, Studio 54's mix of high fashion and street extravagance. The silhouette is fluid and slinky; materials shimmer. The social function is desire and visibility.

**Cultural context:** Gay liberation; Studio 54 as cultural center; post-Stonewall nightlife; Black and Latino dance communities.

**Aesthetic opposites / contrasts:** Punk's anti-polish; hippie earth tones
