# Psychobilly

**Style ID:** `psychobilly`  
**Active Window:** 1980–1989  
**Origin:** UK / US rockabilly circuits  
**Decade(s):** 1980s

## Overview

A speeded-up rockabilly-punk hybrid with towering quiffs, creepers, leopard print, and horror-themed customization.

**Cultural context:** 50s nostalgia remixed through punk aggression and dark humor.

**Aesthetic opposites / contrasts:** Minimalism (90s); preppy / ivy.
