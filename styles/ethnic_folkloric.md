# Ethnic Folkloric

**Style ID:** `ethnic_folkloric`  
**Active Window:** 1966–1972  
**Origin:** US/UK counterculture circuits  
**Decade(s):** 1960s–1970s

## Overview

Caftans, Afghan coats, peasant blouses, embroidery, and global textile motifs folded into western street dress. The aesthetic embraced handmade irregularity and non-western reference systems.

**Cultural context:** Counterculture spirituality; travel routes; anti-industrial values.

**Aesthetic opposites / contrasts:** Space age futurism; power dressing.
